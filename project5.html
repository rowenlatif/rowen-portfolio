Visual Novel Game

This emotionally-driven visual novel follows Theo as he navigates the lingering effects of a past relationship. Players guide his choices after he stumbles upon a powerful reminder of what once was, with each decision shaping his emotional journey and determining one of two possible endings. The project explores themes of guilt, memory, and personal growth through a branching narrative and immersive visual storytelling.

Interactive Storytelling | UI/UX Design | Game Development

2024

Project Purpose

THE PROBLEM

I wanted to build a narrative game that didn’t just tell a story, but invited players to feel it, especially the emotional complexity of loss and healing. The challenge was creating a dialogue system where every choice felt organic and impactful, without making the structure feel rigid or overly game-like.

THE SOLUTION

I developed a branching dialogue engine using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, where player choices influence not only the narrative flow but also Theo’s internal emotional landscape. I created all art assets—character sprites, animations, backgrounds, and UI—by hand using Procreate and Photoshop. I used color theory, lighting shifts, and scene composition to subtly communicate Theo’s emotional state and guide player empathy. The UI was designed to be clean and emotionally immersive, helping the player focus on the story.

THE TAKEAWAY

This project deepened my understanding of interactive narrative design and how to use visual cues to enhance emotional storytelling. I also learned how to structure nonlinear dialogue and manage branching paths in a way that feels natural to the player. It reinforced how even small design decisions, like a shift in color or lighting, can carry emotional weight in an interactive experience.
