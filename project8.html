Kaleidoscope Graphics

As part of the Khoury Student Council’s Marketing and Software Team, I designed graphics that supported events, outreach, and community engagement efforts within Northeastern's Khoury College of Computer Sciences. My work ranged from event promotions to internal branding assets, all created to maintain visual cohesion while reflecting the student-led, tech-forward spirit of Khoury. Each piece was designed with accessibility, clarity, and energy in mind—using bold visuals and adaptable formats for both print and digital use.
